Call For Papers

The Forum for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (FIAM) is an association of persons in India to promote research in mathematical Science. It is started by a group of faculty members working in different reputed institutes. FIAM will act as a major forum for presentation of technological progress and research outcomes in the area of Industrial and Applied Mathematics for researchers from academia and industry. Following the last five conferences from 2018 to 2022 with participants from numerous countries, It is a matter of great pleasure to invite you to 6th edition of the prestigious International Conference on Frontiers in Industrial and Applied Mathematics (FIAM-2023) organized by Birla Institue of Technology and Science Pilani, Dubai Campus on December 21-22, 2023. The conference program will include keynote  speeches, high-quality research paper presentations ad discussion panel in social science. We invite academics, PhD students and professionals to contribute to the conference by presenting papers and/or participating in discussion forums. We would like to particularly encourage submission falling under one of the following conference tracks. Selected papers will be published in Scopus/API indexed journals.

About the Conference

About the Conference

The conference programme will feature:

  • forum for the exchange of ideas
  • fruitful interactions giving rise to new directions
  • insights by mathematicians, engineers, and young researchers who are active in the areas of applied and industrial mathematics.
  • high-quality research paper presentations in concurrent sessions,

The conference will cover the latest research findings, current practices, and theoretical developments in the field of industrial and applied mathematics. We invite academics and professionals to contribute to the conference by presenting papers, participating in the discussion forums, and attending the workshops.

Submissions

How to Submit

Please submit full papers or extended abstracts by 20 October 2023 via EasyChair. To submit, please use the following link: 

https://easychair.org/conferences?conf=fiam2023

The papers could be submitted in any format and layout as a Word or PDF file. The extended abstract should be at least 1000 words long. Please state the track number during the submission and on the top of the first page of your research manuscript. All submissions will go through a review process. Participants with accepted full papers or extended abstracts will be invited to present their papers at the conference.
In case of acceptance, the author receives the Confirmation of Acceptance, which clearly states that the contribution is accepted into the conference programme. Please note that you will receive the notification of acceptance via e-mail within a week of submission.
